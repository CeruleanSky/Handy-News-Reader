"Staying in touch with all Your Interests never has been so easy"

Handy News Reader is a user-friendly, feature rich and customizable free (no adds) open source feed reader.

<b>Features</b>

• OPML export/import
• automatic backup
• offline support
• mark articles to read them later
• gestures for navigation, marking and brightness adjust
• switching articles by volume buttons or swipe, tap areas
• remembering the position of reading per article
• no need to use external web-browser to reach full text
• customize: colors, footer-clock, margins, font sizes, progress bar...
• notifications
• filters and rules
• easy adding google news topics
• organize feeds in folders
• and much more
